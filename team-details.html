B Sreekrishna

Co-founder

Sreekrishna started off his career as an e-commerce technology specialist two decades ago. He went on to become CMD of one of the leading wireless telecom product companies in India – Pointred Telecom Ltd., clocking revenues & order pipelines of more than US $100 Million under his leadership. He was instrumental in creating a wireless product R&D roadmap for the company that resulted in the company acquiring the largest order of WiMAX roll out in India. He also led Computer Access Pvt. Ltd. - a System Integrator and IT services company. One of the key projects that Sreekrishna directly handled was rolling out IT infrastructure for 2085 Schools for a State Government in India. This project was a 5 year Build-operate-Transfer model project valued at US $15 Million.

Sreekrishna is currently a CEO & Co-Founder of DataVector Tech Pvt. Ltd., which is a new age cloud computing company having expertise in DevOps, AI & ML. Sreekrishna is an Engineer in Computer Science from PICT, Pune and Exec. Masters in International Business from IIFT, New Delhi.

Rohit Pande

Co-founder

Rohit has over 2 decades of experience as an entrepreneur in the edtech space in India. He is a pioneer who founded classteacher.com in early 2000 which works with 1.5 million students across 1200 schools in India. classteacher.com works in the field of teacher development and teaching materials, assessment and self learning apps. He worked earlier with Andersen Consulting in Germany after completing his MBA from IIM Calcutta and B.Tech from IIT Delhi.

He is also an active angel investor and sits on the Board of the popular Questt homework app ( questt.com ) as one of his investments. He is also the Managing trustee of iwanttolearnbetterfoundation (iwanttolearnbetter.com)

Sumit Sharma

Co-founder

Sumit is an expert in advanced computing & AI/ML Algorithms. He has over two decades of ASIC designing experience with top VLSI MNCs (ie. Qualcomm, STMicroelectronics, ST-Ericsson). Sumit has a proven track record of building high performing engineering teams. Under his leadership more than 70 state-of-the-art chips are being successfully designed and shipped to premium customers (Apple, Microsoft, Samsung, Nintendo, Sony, HP etc.). During his career he has architected several algorithms and methodology Solutions for engineering productivity and design quality improvements those are still in use in the organizations.

Sumit provides technology consultancy to classteacher.com, he has made a significant contribution to the architecture definition of 2xcell Al Engine.

Sumit earned his post-graduation degree in Physics from Indian Institute of Technology, Delhi (IITD, 1999).
