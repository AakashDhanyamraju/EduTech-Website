2Xcell is an A.I driven self-paced learning and online education platform that helps a student become a ‘Super Learner’ using Hybrid and Blended Learning Model. Students get “Exclusive Personalized Learning where they are guided by “Dedicated Mentor” which strengthens the learning base of students and helps them learn in online courses through customized interactive 3D-2D videos at a much faster pace.
